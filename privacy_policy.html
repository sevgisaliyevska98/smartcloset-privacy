Privacy Policy — Smart Closet

Last updated: November 2025

Smart Closet is a personal outfit management app that helps users organize their wardrobe and create outfit combinations. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and store images you upload of your clothing items. No personal information (such as name, email, or location) is required to use the app.

2. How We Use Information

Uploaded images are used locally within your device to generate outfit combinations. We do not share or sell your data to any third parties.

3. Data Storage

All images and data remain stored locally on your device. No data is sent to our servers.
